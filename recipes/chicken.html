Herb-Grilled Lemon Chicken

Description

Juicy, flavorful, and easy to prepare, herb-grilled lemon chicken is a dependable dinner that's perfect for any night of the week. A simple marinade of fresh lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and herbs infuses the chicken with bright, savory flavor while helping it stay tender and moist on the grill. The result is beautifully charred chicken with a fresh, zesty finish.

This recipe pairs well with grilled vegetables, roasted potatoes, rice pilaf, or a crisp garden salad, making it a versatile option for family dinners or outdoor gatherings. Letting the chicken rest for a few minutes after grilling helps lock in the juices, ensuring every bite is tender and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts (or 6 chicken thighs)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • Lemon wedges, for serving
